Welcome to Niner Rater

Rate Your Professors

Niner Rater is designed to help UNC Charlotte students assess their professors. With our app, you can create reviews and save them in our in-memory database. Plus, you can search and view ratings in a variety of ways. You can search by any combination of name, class number, or subject. All search fields can be used, or you can select certain fields to input. Plus, the average rating of the search results will be displayed.

If you want to view more information about a particular rating, simply click on the individual submission to see all of the information provided with the rating, including the user who submitted it.

Ratings can be searched in multiple ways. A basic search can be performed through the All Ratings page, but a more detailed search baised on multiple combinations of fields can be performed at the Search page.

Best of all, you can view ratings without logging in, but if you want to create a rating, you'll need to log in with your UNC Charlotte Google account.
